A kaleidoscopic blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures, sweetened with sun, sea, sand, seafood, susegad and spirituality, Goa is India's pocket-sized paradise.

Day wise plan:


Day 1

Arrival Kerala

Upon arrival at Kerala Airport/ Railway station, Kerala is one of the most sought-after destinations in India. Later, check into the hotel (Standard check-in time 02:00 pm). If time permits, after refreshment, visit places like the serene backwaters of Alleppey and the beautiful hill station of Munnar. Thereafter, return to the hotel and enjoy an overnight stay at a hotel in scenic Kerala.

Day 2

North Kerala Sightseeings

In the morning, after having breakfast, proceed to visit Fort Kochi. Fort Kochi is a historic area with colonial Portuguese architecture, including the iconic St. Francis Church and the Chinese Fishing Nets, overlooking the Arabian Sea. Then visit Vypin Island and Cherai Beach, known for its tranquil waters and scenic beauty. Later, proceed to visit Mattancherry Palace and Jew Town. Return to the hotel and enjoy an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3

South Goa Sightseeing

In the morning, after breakfast, proceed to visit Mattancherry Palace, Paradesi Synagogue, St. Francis Church, and Santa Cruz Basilica. Explore the local markets of Fort Kochi, visit a Spice Plantation to learn about Kerala's spice heritage, and enjoy the scenic beauty of Cherai Beach. Later, return to the hotel and enjoy an overnight stay.

Day 4

Departure

In the morning, after breakfast, check out from the hotel (Standard check-out time 11:00 am) and transfer to Kochi Airport/ Railway station for your return journey. Depart with beautiful memories.
